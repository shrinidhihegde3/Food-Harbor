OUR MISSION

Our mission is to reduce food waste and alleviate hunger by connecting surplus food with those in need through our innovative donation app. We strive to create a more sustainable and equitable food system by empowering individuals and businesses to easily donate excess food to local charities and community organizations. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those facing food insecurity. Join us in our mission to build a world where no one goes hungry.

ABOUT US

At FOOD HARBOR, we are passionate about fighting food waste and hunger in our communities. Our team is dedicated to creating a platform that makes it easy for individuals and businesses to donate surplus food to those in need. With a commitment to sustainability and social responsibility, we aim to build a community where everyone has access to nutritious food and where no edible food goes to waste. Join us in making a positive impact and helping to create a world where hunger is a thing of the past.
